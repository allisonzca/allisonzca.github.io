Newbody

2020

❮ The Cave
Eduo ❯

“Newbody” is a series of digital posters and fake mobile ads produced by a made-up company called “Nexline,” which link to an application form for a Newbody, a customizable, artificial body in which the user’s mind is transferred to in order to operate as his/her own. Created for the fictional design project in 5D fundamentals to design a product which addresses fictional values. Photographs sourced from Unsplash.
