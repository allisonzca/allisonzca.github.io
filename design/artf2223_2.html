An individual project for 5D Fundamentals to create a site specific design which integrates and/or critiques the site where it is built. My project, "The Cave" is recreation of Plato's Allegory of the Cave in the context of American High School and its academic culture, for which I made a series of slides visualizing its construction and metaphors. The animation is a frame-by-frame animation created in FireAlpaca, and the graphics were created in Adobe Photoshop.
